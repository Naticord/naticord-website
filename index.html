Naticord is...

The point is, Naticord is a native Discord client made using C# and Windows Forms that's open source and doesn't suck.

Naticord doesn't track your every move and send that data to Discord, therefore making it faster and also keeping your privacy.

Naticord is designed to blend seamlessly with native Windows applications, adopting the look and feel of system apps like the Control Panel.
